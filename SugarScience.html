Sugar Science

The only taste humans are born craving is sugar.

Sugar can be used as rocket fuel

Tooth decay isn’t caused by sugar, it’s caused by the bacteria that feed off the sugar. These bacteria will actually feed off any carbohydrate, so starchy foods that can get stuck to your teeth can be just as damaging.

Sugar raises the level of neurotransmitters: dopamine, serotonin, and norepinephrine.

Sugars are the building blocks of carbohydrates, the most abundant type of organic molecules in living things.

Glycolaldehyde, an eight-atom sugar, has even been found in an interstellar gas cloud near the center of the Milky Way.
